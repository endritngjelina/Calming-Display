Shopping Mall Experience - Guide

Time of Day: Changes the lighting and ambiance of the mall. Morning is bright and fresh, evening is warm and cozy, night creates a serene atmosphere.

Atmosphere: Adjust weather conditions. Clear provides optimal visibility, cloudy adds softness, rainy creates a moody atmosphere.

Scenery Zones: Explore different areas of the mall, each with unique characteristics and visual elements.

Crowd Density: Control how many shoppers populate the mall. More crowds create energy, fewer creates calm.

Lighting Intensity: Fine-tune the brightness to your preference.

Animation Speed: Slow down for meditation, speed up for liveliness.

Tips: Try combining evening time with low crowd density for maximum relaxation. Experiment with different zones to discover your favorite view!
